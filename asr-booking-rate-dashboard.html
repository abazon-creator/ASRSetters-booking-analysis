Reading the bars. The solid bar is the booking rate achieved. The orange band is the shortfall to the 40% target — the wider the orange, the bigger the gap. Agents at or above target show no orange at all.

Sources. Sets from the ASR Closer Report Form Responses tab, counted as distinct Email address per ASR per day. Connected calls from the Aloware call-transcript exports where Talk Time ≥ 00:01:30. Agents joined to teams through the roster workbook schedule tab on Aloware Name and Call Tracker Name; where an agent is absent from the schedule the set form's own ASR Team column is used — the two agree on all five agents present in both.

Coverage. 14,851 calls and 690 sets across 54 agents, 14–21 July 2026. 13 July is missing — that export exceeds the 10 MB connector download limit, and it was the heaviest day of the period. Team C and Support show sets but no calls: their agents do not appear in this call-log export, so no rate is computed for them.

On the 43.56% in the Closer Report. That figure divides a full month of sets by an incomplete 90 sec Calls denominator — 21 and 22 July are recorded as zero while sets keep accruing, which is what produces weekly rates above 100%. Measured consistently over the same window, the enterprise rate is 30.0%.
